## Azure Accelerate: Factory Programs (NOT RECOMMENDED)

> ⚠️ **REAL-WORLD EXPERIENCE:** Factory programs are tedious to administer with limited practical benefit. The coordination overhead with Microsoft Factory team outweighs the value of "free" expert resources. **Recommend focusing on paid SMB programs (AI Apps, Data Platform) instead** where you get direct payment and full delivery control.

**Partner Requirement:** Any one Azure Solutions Partner designation (Infrastructure, Data & AI, or Digital & App Innovation)

**Customer Eligibility:** Majors, SMC-Corporate, and select SMB customers (Strategic accounts excluded)

| Activity | Focus Area | Timeline | Partner Payment | Recommendation |
|----------|------------|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Factory Analytics** | Power BI migrations, Fabric lakehouse | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |
| **Factory App & Database Migration** | Windows/Linux, SQL Server | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |
| **Factory Azure VMware Solution** | VMware to AVS migration | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |
| **Factory Gen AI Assistant** | GenAI Solution Accelerator, AOAI | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |
| **Factory Infrastructure Migration** | VM rehost, Azure Arc | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |
| **Factory Secure Azure Cloud** | Defender, Sentinel, Splunk | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |
| **Factory VDI Migration** | RDS/Citrix to AVD | 200 days | $0 | ⚠️ Skip - high admin overhead |

**Why Factory Programs Are Challenging:**
- High coordination overhead with Microsoft Factory team
- Limited flexibility in delivery approach
- Administrative burden outweighs "free" expert value
- Better to self-deliver with SMB program payments ($2K-$12K)

**When Factory MIGHT Make Sense (Rare):**
- Very large, complex migration you cannot staff internally
- Customer specifically requires MS involvement
- First-time delivery in a new technology area (learning opportunity)

**Better Alternative:** Use paid SMB programs (AI Apps, Data Platform) for direct payment and full delivery control.

## Claiming Process

### Eligibility Requirements
- Must hold active Solutions Partner designation
- Customer must have valid TPID detected by Microsoft
- Customer engagement must be documented
- Proof of Execution (POE) required for each activity

### Documentation Required (POE)
1. Infrastructure/Application Compatibility Assessment/Review
2. Landing Zone Setup or Review documentation
3. Deployment evidence of workloads
4. Customer survey completion
5. Partner survey completion

### Submission Timeline
- **Execution window:** 120 days (SMB) or 200 days (Factory)
- Submit claims via Partner Center within execution window
- Allow **30 days** for Microsoft validation
- Payment issued within **45 days** of approval
