## PART 2: UNDERSTANDING THE DIFFERENCES

### Immersion Briefings (1:Many)

**What They Are:**
- **90-minute** group events
- **5-25 customers** attending simultaneously
- **Virtual or in-person**
- Demand generation focus
- Microsoft-subsidized marketing events

**Business Model:**
- Partner hosts event (breakfast briefing, lunch & learn, evening session)
- Invite multiple prospects from your pipeline
- Deliver standardized Microsoft content
- Generate leads for multiple customers at once
- Get paid once for multiple customer touchpoints

**Payment:**
- **$1,500-$2,000 per event** (not per customer)
- One payment covers all customers in attendance
- **Limit: 3 events per year maximum**

**Best Use:**
- Top-of-funnel demand generation
- Build awareness across multiple prospects
- Cost-efficient way to reach many customers
- Generate qualified leads for deeper engagements

**Example:**
- Host breakfast briefing on "Microsoft Security E5"
- Invite 15 prospects from your territory
- Deliver 90-minute Threat Protection Immersion Briefing
- Qualify 5-8 serious prospects for follow-on workshops
- Get paid: **$2,000** (not $2,000 × 15 customers)
- Follow-up: Convert 3 qualified prospects to $8K Envisioning Workshops

---

### Envisioning Workshops (1:1)

**What They Are:**
- **Multi-day** consultative engagements
- **Single customer** focus
- Deep-dive into customer environment
- Technical proof of value
- Production environment testing

**Duration:**
- Typically 2-5 days onsite/virtual
- Hands-on technical work
- Discovery + demonstration + roadmap

**Payment:**
- **$5,500-$10,000** per customer
- Fixed payment based on market tier
- **Limit: Generally 1 per customer per year**

**Best Use:**
- Mid-funnel prospect qualification
- After immersion briefing follow-up
- Technical validation before purchase
- Building business case for customer
- Converting interest into commitment

**Example:**
- Customer attended your Threat Protection briefing
- They want to see it working in their environment
- Deliver 3-day Threat Protection Envisioning Workshop
- Use their production data to show real threats
- Create roadmap for E5 Security deployment
- Get paid: **$8,000**
- Follow-up: Convert to deployment project (separate engagement)

---

### Envisioning & POC Programs (1:1, Extended)

**What They Are:**
- **45-100 hours** of pre-sales work
- **Single customer** engagement
- Proof of Concept deployment
- Business requirements analysis
- Solution design and planning

**Duration:**
- Multi-week engagements
- Combination of assessment + POC + training
- More comprehensive than workshops

**Payment:**
- **$5,000-$25,000** depending on size and scope
- Based on minimum hours delivered
- **Limit: 1 per customer per engagement type** (but can stack different types)

**Best Use:**
- Complex solution validation
- Large enterprise opportunities
- Before major platform purchase
- Competitive displacement scenarios
- Building executive buy-in

**Example:**
- Enterprise customer considering Copilot deployment
- Deliver 100-hour Copilot + Power Envisioning & POC (Medium)
- Build custom agents, deploy pilot to 50 users
- Train administrators and power users
- Create adoption plan
- Get paid: **$25,000**
- Follow-up: Convert to full deployment (separate engagement)

## PART 4: CUSTOMER ELIGIBILITY & LIMITS

### Understanding "1 Per Customer Per Year" Rules:

**Threat Protection Envisioning Workshop:**
- Customer may not have attended between July 1, 2024 - June 1, 2025
- **Meaning:** If customer did workshop in FY25, they can't do it again in FY26
- **Exception:** Different workshop types are separate
  - Customer can do Threat Protection workshop AND Data Security workshop in same year
  - Just can't repeat the SAME workshop type

**Immersion Briefing Limits:**
- **3 events per year per partner** (not per customer)
- You can only run 3 briefing events total in the fiscal year
- Each event can have 5-25 different customers
- Once you hit 3 events, no more until next fiscal year

**Envisioning & POC Limits:**
- **Cloud Endpoints:** 1 per customer tenant max
- **Secure AI Productivity:** 1 per customer tenant max
- **Copilot + Power:** Max 4 approved claims per customer tenant
  - BUT different sizes (XS, S, M) count separately
  - Example: Customer can do XS ($5K) then later upgrade to M ($25K)

---

### Stacking Multiple Programs on Same Customer:

**Allowed:**
✅ Threat Protection Immersion Briefing (customer attends)
✅ THEN → Threat Protection Envisioning Workshop (same customer, 1:1)
✅ THEN → Deployment project (separate engagement)

**Allowed:**
✅ Copilot + Power Envisioning XS ($5K)

✅ Total from one customer: $30K in pre-sales + deployment fees

**NOT Allowed:**
❌ Threat Protection Envisioning Workshop Year 1
❌ Threat Protection Envisioning Workshop Year 2 (same customer - blocked)

**Workaround:**
✅ Threat Protection Envisioning Workshop Year 1
✅ Data Security Envisioning Workshop Year 2 (different workshop type - allowed)
✅ Modern SecOps Envisioning Workshop Year 3 (different workshop type - allowed)

## PART 5: PAYMENT STRUCTURES EXPLAINED

### Fixed Payment Programs:

**Immersion Briefings:**
- Flat $1,500-$2,000 per event
- No variable calculation
- Same payment regardless of how many customers attend (within 5-25 range)
- Payment based on your partner location market tier

**Envisioning Workshops:**
- Flat $5,500-$10,000 per workshop
- No variable calculation
- Based on workshop type and market tier
- Payment not tied to customer purchase (pre-sales funding)

**Envisioning & POC:**
- Flat payment based on engagement size
- Tied to minimum hours delivered
- XS/S/M/L tiers with corresponding payments
- Must meet minimum hour thresholds

---

### Variable Payment Programs:

**ERP/CRM Envisioning Workshops:**
- **Lower of two calculations:**
  1. Hours delivered × Market rate per hour
  2. 7.5% of MSX Opportunity Size (annual revenue opportunity)

**Example 1: Hours-based wins**
- Deliver 30 hours of workshop
- Market A rate: $163/hour
- Calculation: 30 × $163 = **$4,890**
- MSX Opportunity: $100K
- 7.5% of $100K = $7,500
- **You get: $4,890** (lower of the two)

**Example 2: Opportunity-based wins**
- Deliver 50 hours of workshop
- Market A rate: $163/hour
- Calculation: 50 × $163 = $8,150
- **BUT maximum cap = $6,000**
- MSX Opportunity: $50K
- 7.5% of $50K = **$3,750**
- **You get: $3,750** (lower of the two, under cap)

**Example 3: Hit the cap**
- Deliver 40 hours of workshop
- Market A rate: $163/hour
- Calculation: 40 × $163 = $6,520
- **Cap at $6,000**
- MSX Opportunity: $200K
- 7.5% of $200K = $15,000
- **You get: $6,000** (hit the cap)

**Strategy for Variable Programs:**
- Estimate customer opportunity size
- Calculate optimal hours to deliver
- Don't over-deliver hours if opportunity % is limiting
- Don't under-deliver if hours can get you more (up to cap)

## PART 6: CLAIMING PROCESS

### Immersion Briefing Claiming:

**Stage 0: Identify TPID**
- Get customer tenant IDs for all attending companies
- Minimum 5 TPIDs required
- Maximum 25 TPIDs allowed

**Stage 1: Claim Customers (30 days)**
- Add all customer TPIDs to engagement
- Partner Center validates each customer eligibility
- All must meet minimum seat requirements

**Stage 2: Customer Consent (30 days)**
- Consent requested from ALL customers
- Not all need to consent, but minimum 5 must consent
- If below 5 consents, claim expires

**Stage 3: Execute Event (90 days from consent)**
- Deliver 90-minute briefing
- All consenting customers must attend (or representatives)
- Gather proof: attendance records, photos, customer surveys

**Stage 4: Submit POE**
- Upload attendance documentation
- Complete partner survey
- Customer surveys (automated)
- Submit within 90 days

**Stage 5: Payment (30 days validation + 45 days payment)**
- Microsoft validates attendance
- Approves claim
- Payment issued

---

### Envisioning Workshop Claiming:

**Stage 1: Claim Customer (30 days)**
- Nominate single customer
- System validates customer eligibility
- Checks if customer already did this workshop in past year

**Stage 2: Customer Consent (30 days)**
- Customer receives consent request
- Must consent within 30 days
- If no consent, claim expires

**Stage 3: Execute Workshop (90 days from consent)**
- Deliver multi-day workshop
- Complete all required modules
- Work in customer production environment
- Gather technical proof and customer feedback

**Stage 4: Submit POE (within 90 days of consent)**
- Upload workshop deliverables
- Architecture diagrams
- Discovery findings
- Roadmap documents
- Customer survey completion

**Stage 5: Payment (30 days validation + 45 days)**
- Microsoft reviews POE
- Validates workshop completion
- Approves and pays

---

### Envisioning & POC Claiming:

**Similar to workshop claiming but:**
- Longer execution window (varies by program)
- More extensive POE requirements
- Proof of hours delivered
- POC deployment evidence
- Training completion documentation

## PART 8: COMMON QUESTIONS

### Q: Can I do multiple immersion briefings in one year?
**A:** YES, but maximum 3 total events per year. You could do:
- 1× Threat Protection Immersion Briefing
- 1× Data Security Immersion Briefing  
- 1× (third briefing of your choice)
- After 3 events, you're done until next fiscal year

### Q: Can the same customer attend multiple briefings?
**A:** YES. A customer can attend your Threat Protection briefing AND your Data Security briefing. They're different topics. But you only get paid once per EVENT, not per customer.

### Q: If I do an Envisioning Workshop with a customer, can I do it again next year?
**A:** DEPENDS on the specific workshop. Many have "customer may not have attended in past 12 months" rules. Check specific engagement terms.

### Q: Can I stack an Immersion Briefing + Envisioning Workshop for same customer?
**A:** YES! Excellent strategy:
- Customer attends briefing (you get $2K for the event)
- Customer wants deeper dive
- Deliver 1:1 Envisioning Workshop (you get $8K)
- Total: $10K from one customer + event revenue

### Q: What's the timeline from briefing to payment?
**A:** Immersion Briefing: ~4-5 months
- Month 1: Plan and nominate
- Month 2: Get consents and execute event
- Month 3: Submit POE
- Month 4: Validation
- Month 5: Payment

**A:** Envisioning Workshop: ~5-6 months
- Month 1: Nominate customer
- Month 2: Get consent, start workshop
- Month 3: Complete workshop, submit POE
- Month 4: Validation
- Month 5: Payment

### Q: Can I charge the customer for these workshops?
**A:** YES. The Microsoft funding is a CO-INVESTMENT. You can (and should) charge the customer additional fees for:
- Extra hours beyond minimum
- Custom deliverables
- Extended POC period
- Additional training
- Implementation planning beyond scope

**Example:**
- Microsoft pays: $8,000 (Envisioning Workshop)
- You charge customer: $15,000 (for comprehensive assessment)
- Total revenue: $23,000
- Customer's net cost: $15,000 (they don't see Microsoft's $8K)

### Q: What if customer doesn't proceed after workshop?
**A:** You still get paid! These are PRE-SALES programs. Payment is for delivering the workshop/briefing, not for customer purchase. However, Microsoft tracks your success rate - if too many customers don't proceed, you may be paused from program.

### Q: Do I need to be CSP to claim workshop funding?
**A:** DEPENDS:
- Security/Modern Work workshops: NO CSP requirement
- Business Applications workshops: YES, must be CSP Direct or Indirect Reseller (for most)
- Check specific engagement eligibility

### Q: Can I subcontract the workshop delivery?
**A:** NO. Same rule as project engagements - all work must be delivered by your team directly.
