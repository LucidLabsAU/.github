## RISK MITIGATION

### **Risk: Low Attendance**
- **Mitigation:** Over-invite 3:1 ratio (invite 15-20 to get 5-7)
- **Mitigation:** Multiple reminder emails
- **Mitigation:** Offer recording to registrants who can't attend live
- **Fallback:** Minimum 3 attendees to proceed, otherwise reschedule

### **Risk: Low Engagement During Session**
- **Mitigation:** Interactive elements (polls, Q&A throughout)
- **Mitigation:** Keep to 60-75 minutes max (not full 90)
- **Mitigation:** Engaging speaker (practice delivery)
- **Fallback:** Follow up individually even if session is quiet

### **Risk: Poor Conversion to Follow-up**
- **Mitigation:** Clear CTA at end ("Free 30-min consultation")
- **Mitigation:** Survey with "I'm interested in follow-up" checkbox
- **Mitigation:** Personal email from presenter (not generic)
- **Fallback:** Nurture for 30-60 days, try again

### **Risk: Time Investment Doesn't Pay Off**
- **Mitigation:** Reuse content across multiple briefings
- **Mitigation:** Record and use as marketing asset
- **Mitigation:** Set strict limits (max 2 briefings, then evaluate)
- **Fallback:** Stop after February if no traction

## THEME SELECTION GUIDE

### **When to Lead with Data & AI:**
- Customer mentions "reporting," "analytics," "business intelligence"
- Has multiple databases or data silos
- Paying high SQL Server/Oracle licensing fees
- Wants to "do something with AI" but doesn't know what
- Has data team or analysts on staff

### **When to Lead with Agentic AI:**
- Customer mentions "automation," "chatbot," "customer service"
- Has repetitive manual processes
- Government sector (efficiency focus)
- Has development team or budget
- Asking about ChatGPT or generative AI

### **When to Lead with Infrastructure:**
- Customer mentions "VMware," "servers," "disaster recovery"
- Hardware refresh cycle coming up
- Remote work pain points
- Security or compliance concerns
- IT operations focus (not business/data focus)

### **When to Combine Themes:**
- Enterprise customer ($100K+ Azure potential)
- Long-term strategic relationship
- Multiple pain points across themes
- Multi-year transformation journey
- C-level engagement and sponsorship
